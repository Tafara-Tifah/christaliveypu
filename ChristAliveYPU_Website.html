Who We Are

About YPU

Christ Alive Young People Union

We are the youth department of Christ Alive Assembly, under the Apostolic Faith Mission of Zimbabwe (AFMOZ). A community of young believers committed to growing in faith, serving one another and reaching our generation for Christ.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of young people who are rooted in the Word of God, active in their community, and passionate about the Kingdom of Christ.

Our Vision

A vibrant, Spirit-filled youth department that transforms lives and shapes the future of the Church in Zimbabwe and beyond.
